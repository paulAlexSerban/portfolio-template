# Templates
- Complete layouts and wireframes which include atoms, molecules, and organisms. A template starts to resemble parts of a webpage or mobile application.
- skeleton of your page, made up of groups of organisms and/or molecules to form the common structure of a page - for example, a template for the articles on your website (blog-post) and another for the sign forms (register)
